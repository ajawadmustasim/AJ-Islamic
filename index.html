About AJ Islamic

My name is Ahmad Jawad. I am Pakistani Science Student. I am sharing whatever I have learned from Quran, Hadees and daily life experiences.

I am only discussing things that are naseeha (advices) proved from consensus and perpetual practices. I avoid discussing problems of jurisprudence.

I am sharing Islamic content on this channel with references. References of Ahadees are according to the international numbering from AhleSunnat Hadees books.

Quality of videos is Ultra HD (1080 p). Videos are generally in Urdu language however English Translation of videos is also available. The sound effects used in videos are vocals only (nasheed vocals) not music.
